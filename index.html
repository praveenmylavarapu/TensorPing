TENSOR PING RULES

TensorPing is a daily logic puzzle where you deduce a secret mathematical network connecting Node IN to Node OUT through Nodes H1 and H2.

You have 6 actions to solve the puzzle. You can perform two types of actions:

1. SEND PULSE: Fire a numerical test value (1-20) from Node IN. Watch the signal travel and read the final output at Node OUT. This consumes 1 action.

2. SUBMIT GUESS: Guess the three mathematical operations for the connections: OP 1 (IN → H1), OP 2 (H1 → H2), and OP 3 (H2 → OUT). This consumes 1 action.

FEEDBACK CHIPS: After submitting a guess, you will receive feedback status colors on your operations:

CYAN
Operation and number are correct and at the correct step.
MAGENTA
Operation and number are correct but belong at a different step.
GREY
Operation or number does not exist in the secret network.

Your guess will only be accepted if it matches all your previously recorded pulses.
