TENSORPING RULES

TensorPing™ is a daily logic puzzle where you deduce a secret mathematical network connecting Node N1 to Node N4 through Nodes N2 and N3.

You have 6 actions to solve the puzzle. You can perform two types of actions:

1. SEND PULSE: Fire a numerical test value (1-20) from Node N1. Watch the signal travel and read the final output at Node N4. This consumes 1 action.

2. SUBMIT GUESS: Guess the three mathematical operations between N1 → N2, N2 → N3, and N3 → N4. This consumes 1 action.

FEEDBACK CHIPS: After submitting a guess, you will receive feedback status colors on your operations:

CYAN
Operation and number are correct and at the correct step.
MAGENTA
Operation and number are correct but belong at a different step.
GREY
Operation or number does not exist in the secret network.

Your guess will only be accepted if it matches all your previously recorded pulses.
